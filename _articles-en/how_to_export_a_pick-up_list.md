### **Step 1**

Go to “Manage > Pick-ups > Pick-up Lists”.

### **Step 2**

Choose “Export” in the ellipsis menu.

![How_to_export_a_pick-up_list1](/assets/images/how_to_export_a_pick_up_list1.png)

The pick-up list will be downloaded in ".csv" format.
