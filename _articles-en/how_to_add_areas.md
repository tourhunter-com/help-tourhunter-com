## **Step 1**

Go to “Manage > Pick-ups > Areas”.

## **Step 2**

Click on the “+ New Area” button.

![How_to_add_areas1](/assets/images/how_to_add_areas1.png)

## **Step 3**

Fill the field “Name”.

![How_to_add_areas2](/assets/images/how_to_add_areas2.png)

## **Step 4** 

Click on the “Add” button.
